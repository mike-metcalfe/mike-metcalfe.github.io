About Us

Amber-Lee Lighting Sales is an independant light company with manufacturer's representatives of commercial, architectural and industrial lighting. We specialize in the sale of the industry's finest lighting and related products. Our customer base is made up of Landscape Architects, Lighting Designers, Electrical Engineers, Electrical Wholesalers and Subcontractors. Our geographic territory is Southwestern Ontario. We are able to utilize different manufactures for different needs. We can provide the best overall lighting package to fit any project requirement. Our goal is to provide a professional lighting package with the help of our knowledgeable sales force while maintaining a high level of customer service.

We strive to help you make your project brighter and more beautiful.

Let Amber-Lee Lighting Sales be your preferred partner in Southwestern Ontario. Please call or email us about your next project. We would be happy to help.
